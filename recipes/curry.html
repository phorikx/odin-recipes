Green Curry Recipe

Description

Green Curry is a classic Thai dish, usually served with rice. It uses a curry pasta and coconut milk to create a sauce, which it fills with a source of protein and vegetables.

Ingredients

  • Rice
  • Shallots
  • Garlic
  • Green Peppers
  • Fish Sauce
  • Coriander
  • Ginger
  • Lemongrass
  • Coriander, Mustard and Cumin seeds
  • Coconut Milk
  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Palm sugar

Instructions

  1. Cook rice to doneness and let it fluff up before serving.
  2. Toast the spices. Add to mortar and pestle with lemongrass, shallots, garlic, ginger and green peppers. Grind to a paste.
  3. Fry shallot paste in pan. Chop chicken to pieces and add to pan as well.
  4. Chop vegetables to pieces and fry with chicken after chicken is almost done.
  5. Add coconut milk and let simmer for a while. Add fish sauce and palm sugar for flavour.
