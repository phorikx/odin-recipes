Lasagna Recipe

Description

Lasagna is a pasta recipe baked in the oven, with cheese on top and a sauce between sheets of pasta, usually one based on tomatoes.

Ingredients

  • Lasagna sheets
  • Carrot
  • Onion
  • Garlic
  • Selery
  • Tomato Paste
  • Short-ribs
  • Canned Peeled Tomatoes

Instructions

  1. Chop the onions, garlic, selery and carrots and sweat them in a pan.
  2. Take out the aromatics, add butter to the pan and brown the shortribs
  3. Add tomato paste and brown
  4. Add the aromatics back in
  5. Add wine and peeled tomatoes
  6. Stew for 4 hours
  7. Remove bones from short ribs
  8. Build pasta with lasagna sheets in an oven-proof tray
  9. Bake for 30 min.
